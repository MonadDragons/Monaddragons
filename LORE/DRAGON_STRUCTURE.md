# DRAGON MON — DRAGON STRUCTURE

## The Universal Blueprint of Every Dragon

Every Dragon Mon follows a standardized structure.

This document defines the official format used for all present and future Dragons.

A Dragon is not only an artwork.

A Dragon is a complete identity composed of:

- Visual identity
- Lore identity
- Metadata identity
- Philosophical identity
- Future intelligence identity

Every Dragon must follow this structure to preserve consistency across the entire ecosystem.

---

# DRAGON IDENTIFICATION

Every Dragon receives a permanent identifier.

Format:

DXXX

Example:

D001

D101

D528

The ID never changes.

The ID represents the Dragon's position inside the Dragon Mon archive.

# VISUAL IDENTITY

Every Dragon contains a fixed visual definition.

---

## Dragon Material

Default:

Ultra-Matte

Rules:

No:

- Texture
- Scales
- Skin
- Gloss
- Metallic reflection
- Noise
- Surface variation

The Dragon remains a timeless geometric symbol.

---

## Dragon Color

Defines the Dragon variation.

Examples:

- Ultra-Matte Sapphire
- Ultra-Matte Emerald
- Ultra-Matte White

## Aura

The energy field behind the Dragon.

Rules:

- Always behind the Dragon
- Never covers the Dragon
- Defines elemental or conceptual energy

- Fire Orange × Solar Gold
- Monad Violet × Infinite White

---

## Eyes

The eyes represent the Dragon's energy.

Rules:

- Minimal
- Symmetrical
- Glowing
- No expression

Examples:

- Crystal Blue

---

## Halo

The circular energy field behind the Dragon.

Purpose:

Represents rarity and identity.

---

## Background

The environment surrounding the Dragon.

Rules:

- Minimal particles

- Objects
